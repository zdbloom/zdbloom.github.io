Chinese Version

Dr. Dong Zhou is currently a professor affiliated with School of Information Science and Technology, Guangdong University of Foreign Studies & Guangzhou Key Laboratory of Intelligent Processing of Less Common Languages. He is a Ph.D./Master Student Supervisor, Young "Furong" Scholar of Hunan Province, Young Backbone Teacher of Hunan Province (the evaluation result is excellent), Xiangtan High-level talent, "Xiangjiang Scholars" Distinguished Professor (overseas Ph.D.) of Hunan University of Science and Technology, "Yunshan" Outstanding Scholar of Guangdong University of Foreign Studies.

He received his M.Sc. degree from the University of York, UK, and his Ph.D. degree from the University of Nottingham, UK. From 2008 to 2012, he was a Research Fellow in the School of Computer Science and Statistics, Trinity College Dublin, Ireland. He has published more than 100 papers in well-known international conferences and journals including IEEE TKDE, ACM Computing Surveys, Chinese Journal of Computers, Journal of Software and IJCAI, CIKM, EMNLP, COLING, etc.

His current research interests include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Information Retrieval
  • Social Media and Social Computing
  • Data Science and Big Data
