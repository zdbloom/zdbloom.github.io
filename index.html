Dr. Dong Zhou is currently a professor affiliated with School of Information Science and Technology, Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. He is a Ph.D./Master Student Supervisor, Young "Furong" Scholar of Hunan Province, Young Backbone Teacher of Hunan Province (the evaluation result is excellent), Xiangtan High-level talent, "Xiangjiang Scholars" Distinguished Professor (overseas Ph.D.) of Hunan University of Science and Technology, "Yunshan" Outstanding Scholar of Guangdong University of Foreign Studies.

He received his M.Sc. degree from the University of York, UK, and his Ph.D. degree from the University of Nottingham, UK. He was a Research Fellow in the School of Computer Science and Statistics, Trinity College Dublin, Ireland. He has published more than 120 papers in well-known international journals and conferences including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TASLP, IEEE TAI, ACM Computing Surveys, ACM TALIP and ACL, IJCAI, CIKM, EMNLP, COLING, etc.

His current research interests include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Big Data Analysis
  • Large Language Models
  • Country Studies in Asia and Africa
  • Asian and African Languages and Cultures
